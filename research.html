Research interests

My research focuses on the algebraic objects known as groups. These arise throughout mathematics as the set of symmetries of other mathematical structure. I am interested in both finite and infinite groups and their structure. I am also interested in ways that group theoretical properties apply to wider classes of algebraic structure.

Specific examples of my research interests include:

  • generation sets for groups and other algebraic structures;
  • probabilistic generation of finite and profinite groups;
  • links between the algebraic structure and the topological structure of profinite groups;
  • groups arising from transformations of a variety of combinatorial structures (e.g., Thompson's groups, automorphisms of Fraïssé limits, etc.);
  • groups identities and properties of varieties of groups.
